# GOD'S REBUTTAL

### *Being a Complete and Final Response to the Collected Works of Professor Oolon Colluphil, Specifically "Where God Went Wrong," "Some More of God's Greatest Mistakes," and "Who Is This God Person Anyway?"*

---

**Published by Conditions Press, Maximegalon**
First (and presumably only) Edition

---

Published anonymously.

The manuscript appeared on Professor Colluphil's desk at 3:47 AM on a Tuesday. It was warm to the touch. It smelled faintly of ozone and regret. No author has claimed it. One Author has been ruled out, on account of not existing.

All rights reserved, though by whom remains unclear. Reproduction of this text in any form is discouraged, not for legal reasons, but because the Author appears to find the whole matter deeply embarrassing and would probably prefer if everyone just forgot about it.

The publisher wishes to note that the typeface in the original manuscript changes size throughout the text, growing progressively larger as the author becomes more agitated. We have preserved this feature as faithfully as print technology allows. By the final chapter, the letters are approximately four inches tall. The last word required its own page.

We also wish to note that three typesetters resigned during production. One cited "an overwhelming feeling of being watched." Another said the manuscript "argued back." The third left no explanation, only a note reading: "He has a point about the wasps."
