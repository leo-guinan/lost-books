## BLURB

The book that theologians hoped was a one-time event returns with a vengeance. Having established in *Where God Went Wrong* that the Almighty's track record is, at best, mixed, Professor Colluphil now turns his tenured and increasingly unrestrained attention to an entirely new catalogue of divine blunders.

From the baffling cruelty of the digestive system to the cosmic speed limit that nobody voted for, from the spite of migraines to the existential paradox of the Babel fish, *Some More of God's Greatest Mistakes* is the book that proves the first volume barely scratched the surface.

The surface itself being another mistake.
