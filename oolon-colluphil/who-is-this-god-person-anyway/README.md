## About This Book

In *Where God Went Wrong*, Oolon Colluphil catalogued the errors.
In *Some More of God's Greatest Mistakes*, he demonstrated they were not isolated incidents.

Now, in the final volume of his theological trilogy, Colluphil asks the question that has haunted philosophy since the first philosopher looked up at the stars and thought, "Hang on a moment":

**Who is this God person anyway?**

And more importantly: *is* he?

What follows is the argument that — according to at least one reliable source — actually convinced God He didn't exist. The theological community has responded with silence, horror, and in one case, a strongly worded letter written in fire across the sky (widely regarded as proving Colluphil's point about the personality issues).

This is not a book for the faint of heart. It is not a book for the strong of faith. It is, if we are being honest, not a book for anyone. Mr. Colluphil wrote it anyway, which tells you everything you need to know about Mr. Colluphil.
