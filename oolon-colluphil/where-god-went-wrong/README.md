## About This Work

In this, the first volume of what would become the most controversial trilogy in galactic theological history, Professor Oolon Colluphil of the University of Maximegalon presents what he modestly describes as "a few notes on the project." Beginning from first principles and working methodically through every major deliverable of Creation, Colluphil constructs an argument so watertight that God Himself was forced to concede the point and promptly vanished in a puff of logic.[^1]

*Where God Went Wrong* is essential reading for anyone who has ever looked at the universe and thought, "Surely someone should have caught this in testing."

[^1]: See also: *Some More of God's Greatest Mistakes* (Vol. 2) and *Who Is This God Person Anyway?* (Vol. 3) for the increasingly personal turn this took.
